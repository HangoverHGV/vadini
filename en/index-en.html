Offered Services

  • We deliver any type of custom-made metal constructions: welded metal structures, industrial parts, prototypes, and custom products for construction and industrial applications.
  • We work rigorously, with complete traceability – from design and WPS/WPQR to the final inspection – ensuring that every piece comes out right the first time; we comply with EN ISO and EN 1090 standards with NDT control and CE marking.
  • Our mission: correct, repeatable, and documented joints, focused on durability and safety; if you have drawings, specifications, or a concept idea, we quickly dive into the technical details and propose the optimal execution and welding solution.
  1. Container rental (construction, waste, demolition, rubble, any type) – site containers and waste containers, with logistics, transport, and pick-up on request.
  2. Container sales – new and second-hand, customization (dimensions, doors, windows), fast delivery.
  3. Excavator bucket repairs by welding – edge reconditioning, hardfacing, tooth replacement, reinforcements, and anti-wear plating.
  4. Construction equipment repairs by welding – arms, chassis, blades, buckets, structural cracks; on-site interventions using qualified procedures.
  5. Fabrication of metal structures – halls, frames, stairs, railings, flooring, platforms, gates, and fences, compliant with EN 1090.
  6. Cutting and welding of all types of materials – steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper; MIG/MAG, TIG, MMA; mechanical cutting (on request: plasma/oxy-fuel).
  7. Any special request – unique parts, small/medium series, prototyping, reverse engineering, assembly, and maintenance.
